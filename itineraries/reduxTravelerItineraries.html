Redux Traveler Itineraries

Highlights:

Four itineraries for touring USA; route maps, mileages, places to visit each day.

Distances include driving to and through the various parks.

Texas New Mexico Chihuahuan Desert National Parks

See the National Parks of Western Texas/ Southeast New Mexico situated around the Chihuahuan Desert- Big Bend National Park, Guadalupe Mountains National Park, Carlsbad Caverns National Park and White Sands National Park.

6 days, 840 miles; starts from El Paso, TX

Southeast USA Appalachians and Historic Cities

Experience the best of the Southern Appalachian Mountains and great Southeastern cities including the most visited National Park in the US- Great Smoky Mountains National Park, art and nature loving Asheville, NC, beautiful views of Appalachia along the Blue Ridge Parkway and Mt Mitchell, South Carolina's wetland park- Congaree National Park, and historic Charleston, SC and Savannah, GA.

7 days, 599 miles; starts from Knoxville, TN

Central California San Fran, Sierras and Big Sur

Enjoy the best of Central California- San Francisco, Lake Tahoe, Yosemite NP, Kings Canyon & Sequoia NP, Pinnacles NP, and the Big Sur area of the Pacific Coast Highway

10 days, 1001 miles; starts from San Francisco, CA

Utah Big 5 National Parks

Marvel at Utah's 5 National Parks- Zion, Bryce Canyon, Capitol Reef, Canyonlands, and Arches, plus several scenic stops including Monument Valley.

8 days, 1086 miles; starts from Las Vegas, NV
