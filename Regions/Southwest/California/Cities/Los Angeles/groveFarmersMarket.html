How to Get There

Head northwest on W 1st St toward N Spring St. Continue onto Beverly Blvd. Turn left onto Reno St. Turn right onto W 3rd St. Turn right onto S Gilmore Ln.

The Lake

Sitting south of West Hollywood is one of LA's most beloved landmarks: The Original Farmers Market. Founded in 1934, this cream-colored facility reels in both residents and tourists with the promise of fresh produce and the aroma of ready-to-eat snacks. Entry is free. keep in mind that this market is often crowded and has limited tables, so expect to eat outside the market area or wait for a table to become available. For a more contemporary shopping experience, head to The Grove. This massive commercial and entertainment complex sits adjacent to The Original Farmers Market. Here, you'll find a long list of your favorite shopping haunts, as well as a variety of dining venues, a movie theater and plenty of special events. once on-site, travelers can soak up The Grove's trendy atmosphere and find plenty to buy and eat.
