Walt Disney Concert Hall

Southwest > California > Los Angeles > Walt Disney Concert Hall

Highlights:

Designed by architect Frank Gehry, Walt Disney Concert Hall is an internationally recognized architectural landmark and one of the most acoustically sophisticated concert halls in the world

General Information

Hours:
Audio tours are offered Monday through Saturday from either 10am to 2pm or 2pm to 5pm, while guided tour hours vary by day and are generally available on Thursdays, Fridays, Saturdays and Sundays
Fees:
Hourlong guided and audio tours are both free
Pet Policy:
No pets allowed

Head northwest on W 1st St toward N Spring St. Turn left onto S Olive St. Turn right onto W 2nd St.
