How to Get There

From Los Angeles (50mi) Get on US-101 S. Follow I-5 S to CA-133 S in Irvine. Take exit 95 from I-5 S. Follow CA-133 S to Broadway St in Laguna Beach.
From San Diego (78mi) Get on I-5 N. Follow I-5 N to El Toro Rd in Aliso Viejo. Take exit 6 from CA-73 N. Take CA-133 S to Broadway St in Laguna Beach

The Mountain

Located between San Diego and Los Angeles, Laguna Beach is situated right along Pacific Coast Highway, making it a perfect stop on your California road trip. The town has a palpable penchant for the arts that can be found not only inside the many galleries that line PCH but also the streets; there are more than 100 public art pieces spread throughout town. So while in Laguna Beach, take time to immerse yourself in the local culture, soak up the coast as much as you can and don't forget to take plenty of pictures along the way. Main Beach is located right in the heart of Laguna. It is located in the Village Area near downtown. This is a wide beach with more sandy area than many of the smaller beaches and coves of Laguna Beach. There are volleyball courts, a lifeguard stand, basketball courts,a boardwalk,a playground, large grassy areas, picnic tables, benches, showers, and a restroom. At the north end, there are tiodepools as well. Main Beach is just south of Rockpile Beach and Heisler Park and just north of Sleepy Hollow Beach. There is metered street parking along Coast HIghway.
