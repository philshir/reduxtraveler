The City

Located along the South Carolina - North Carolina border near the town of Rosman, North Carolina, Sassafras Mountain, is South Carolinaâ€™s highest point at 3,553 feet above sea level. The summit is an easy 300 foot walk from the parking lot and is, by merit of not having stairs, handicapped accessible, although the path is fairly steep. A long-awaited observation tower officially opened to the public on April 22, 2019, Earth Day. The tower offers an unparalleled view of the mountains of South Carolina, North Carolina and Georgia and the Blue Ridge Mountains.
