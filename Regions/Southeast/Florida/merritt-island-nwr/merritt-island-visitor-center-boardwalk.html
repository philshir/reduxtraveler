Merritt Island NWR Visitor Center Boardwalk Trail

Southeast > Florida > Merritt Island NWR > Merritt Island NWR Visitor Center Boardwalk Trail

Highlights:

The Boardwalk offers great views of two freshwater ponds, a native butterfly garden, hammock, a wetland prairie and associated wildlife

General Information

Length:
0.8 miles round trip
Elevation gain:
0 feet
Pet Policy:
Pets allowed
Seasons:
All year though Summer can be very hot and humid. Mosquitoes are less troublesome during Winter months
Trailhead:
Directly behind the Visitors Center
Rating (1-5):
★★★★

The Boardwalk offers great views of two freshwater ponds, a native butterfly garden, hammock, a wetland prairie and associated wildlife. The trail is wheelchair accessible and there are three benches along the boardwalk
