How to Get There

From New York City (73mi) Get on I-78 W/Holland Tunnel from Church St and 6th Ave. Take I-280 W, I-80 W and NJ-15 N to County Rd 565/Ross Corner Sussex Rd in Frankford. Continue on County Rd 565. Take NJ-23 N to Monument Rd in Montague Township

Overview

High Point is a mountain peak within High Point State Park. it is the highest elevation in the state, with a peak elevation of 1,803 feet. The closest city is Port Jervis, New York, which lies to the northwest. Three states – New Jersey, New York, and Pennsylvania – can be seen from the top. At the peak is the High Point Monument, a 220-foot obelisk, built in 1930 as a war memorial. The Monument has 291 steps from the base to the highest viewing platform. The mountain is in the High Point State Park. Route 23 skirts the park and carries visitors from the New Jersey suburbs and from points in New York State.
